Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Dogsica Comic Bot is a personal automation. It collects no data from any third party and has no users other than its operator.

Data Accessed

The Service uses TikTok API access tokens issued by the operator's own TikTok account, solely to publish content the operator created. No personal information about TikTok viewers, followers, or any third party is read, stored, or transmitted.

Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored in Google Apps Script's encrypted Properties Service, accessible only to the operator's Google account.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party.

Contact

Questions: open an issue at https://github.com/dogsica/dogsica.github.io
